Welcome

I am a Research Fellow at Mayo Clinic.

My primary research interests lie in image processing, novel image acquisition design, pathology detection using medical imaging, etc. I develop AI-assisted algorithms and have been focusing on integrating medical imaging physics in the development. My vision is to design robust and generalizable AI algorithms that go beyond data-driven principles.
